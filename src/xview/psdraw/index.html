PSDRAW - PostScript Drawing with XVPS

This is another XView drawing program based on NeWS and XVPS. Unlike painter, this one works. The trick is that, to draw any of the shapes, say a rectangle, you have to click and release the left (SELECT) button in one corner, move to the opposite corner, and click the middle (EXTEND) button. Makes perfect sense in the context of OPEN LOOK.

Try it! (Sun only)
