Catcher, a scripting language

Well, the title tells it. Catcher is a neat scripting language, like Tk but written several years earlier, by Chuck Musciano, originally for SunView and then for XView/OPEN LOOK. Due to the copyright, we were not able to include Catcher at this time, however you can obtain a copy by FTP from many archive sites, including Rain.com. It is on many other sites, so if that site doesn't work, use archie or your favorite Net Searching tool to find a copy.
